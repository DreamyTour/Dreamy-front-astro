# Gender Equality Policy

At Turismo Perú Bolivia, we are committed to gender equality and non-discrimination.

## Our Commitments

- **Equal Opportunities**: We guarantee the same treatment and opportunities for all our employees
- **Safe Environment**: Zero tolerance for sexual harassment and gender discrimination
- **Pay Equity**: Fair wages for equal work, regardless of gender
- **Representation**: We promote equal participation of women at all levels

## Concrete Actions

- Gender equality training programs
- Gender committees to prevent harassment
- Work-family balance policies
- Anonymous reporting channel for incidents

## Report

If you know or experience any discrimination situation, report it to: igualdad@turismo-peru-bolivia.com
